PJ's Top Games 2024

A recap of the best titles of this year

1. Elden Ring: Shadow of the Erdtree

Essentially a sequel to already one of the best games of all time. I can't believe they call this a DLC. The amount of content is ridiculous.

2. Black Myth Wukong

Incredibly fluid gameplay, fun boss fights, and an interesting story.

3. Another Crab's Treasure

A fresh take on the souls-like genre, and a lot of fun.
